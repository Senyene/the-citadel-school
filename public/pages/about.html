About The Citadel School

Excellence in Education Since 1995

Our Story

Our History

Founded in 1995, The Citadel School has been a beacon of educational excellence in Okoria, Bayelsa State. What began as a small nursery school has grown into a comprehensive educational institution serving students from ages 2 through post-secondary education.

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, and lifelong learning, preparing students to be leaders in their communities and the world.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a premier educational institution that develops well-rounded individuals who embody our motto "First Among Equals" - excelling while maintaining humility and respect for all.

Modern facilities designed to provide the best learning environment for our students.
