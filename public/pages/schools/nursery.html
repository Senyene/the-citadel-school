Nursery School

Building strong foundations for children aged 2-5

Early Years Foundation

Our Nursery School follows the Nigerian Early Childhood Care and Education (ECCE) curriculum, providing a nurturing environment that promotes holistic development through play-based learning.

Playgroup

Ages 2-3 years

  • Focus on sensory and motor development
  • Introduction to social skills
  • Basic language acquisition

Pre-Kindergarten

Ages 3-4 years

  • Enhanced language development
  • Introduction to numbers and letters
  • Creative expression through arts

Kindergarten

Ages 4-5 years

  • Preparation for primary school
  • Basic reading and writing skills
  • Introduction to simple mathematics

Our Curriculum

We implement a blend of the Nigerian ECCE framework and Montessori principles:

Language Development

  • English language (Phonics, Storytelling)
  • Introduction to Nigerian languages (Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa)
  • Vocabulary building

Numeracy

  • Number recognition (1-100)
  • Basic counting and sorting
  • Simple addition and subtraction

Science & Discovery

  • Nature exploration
  • Simple experiments
  • Environmental awareness

Creative Arts

  • Drawing and painting
  • Music and movement
  • Nigerian cultural arts

Physical Development

  • Fine motor skills (writing, cutting)
  • Gross motor skills (running, jumping)
  • Coordination exercises

Social & Emotional

  • Sharing and cooperation
  • Nigerian cultural values
  • Self-expression

Typical Daily Schedule

7:30 - 8:00 Arrival & Free Play
