Diploma Programs

Post-secondary vocational and technical education

Career-Focused Education

Our Diploma programs are accredited by the National Board for Technical Education (NBTE) and designed to provide practical skills for immediate employment or entrepreneurship.

Programs Offered

Business Administration

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Certification: ND in Business Admin
  • Specializations: Accounting, Marketing, HR

Computer Science

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Certification: ND in Computer Science
  • Specializations: Programming, Networking

Electrical/Electronics

  • Duration: 2 years
  • Certification: ND in Electrical Engineering
  • Specializations: Power Systems, Electronics

Hospitality Management

  • Duration: 18 months
  • Certification: Professional Diploma
  • Specializations: Hotel Management, Catering

Admission Requirements

  • Minimum of 5 credits in WASSCE/NECO including English and Mathematics
  • Completed application form
  • Birth certificate or sworn declaration of age
  • Medical certificate of fitness
  • Four recent passport photographs
  • Testimonial from last school attended
