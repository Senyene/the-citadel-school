Primary School

Quality foundational education for Grades 1-6

Basic Education

Our Primary School follows the Nigerian Universal Basic Education (UBE) curriculum, providing a solid foundation in core subjects while nurturing creativity and critical thinking.

Lower Primary (Grades 1-3)

  • Focus on literacy and numeracy foundations
  • Introduction to core subjects
  • Development of basic learning skills

Upper Primary (Grades 4-6)

  • Advanced subject knowledge
  • Development of critical thinking
  • Preparation for secondary education

Curriculum Structure

We implement the Nigerian UBE curriculum with enhancements:

Core Subjects

  • English Studies
  • Mathematics
  • Basic Science and Technology
  • Nigerian Languages (Yoruba, Igbo, Hausa)
  • Social Studies

Humanities

  • History of Nigeria
  • Civic Education
  • Cultural and Creative Arts
  • Religious Studies (Christian/Islamic)

Practical Subjects

  • Agricultural Science
  • Home Economics
  • Computer Studies
  • Physical and Health Education

Special Programs

Reading Enhancement

Daily reading sessions to improve literacy

STEM Club

Hands-on science and technology activities

Cultural Exchange

Learning about Nigeria's diverse cultures

Debate & Public Speaking

Developing communication skills

Assessment Approach

Continuous Assessment

40% of total score from classwork, projects, and tests

Terminal Examinations

60% of total score from end-of-term exams

Practical Evaluation

Skills demonstration for practical subjects
