Secondary School

Preparing students for WASSCE and beyond

Junior & Senior Secondary

Our Secondary School follows the Nigerian 9-Year Basic Education Curriculum (BEC) for JSS and the Senior Secondary Curriculum for SS classes, preparing students for WASSCE and university education.

Junior Secondary (JSS 1-3)

  • Broad-based education
  • Core subjects + pre-vocational electives
  • Preparation for BECE

Senior Secondary (SS 1-3)

  • Specialization in Science, Arts or Commercial
  • WASSCE/NECO preparation
  • University entrance exam coaching
