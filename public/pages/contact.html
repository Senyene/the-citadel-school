Frequently Asked Questions

What are your admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by level. Please visit our admissions page for detailed requirements for each school level from nursery to diploma programs.

When does the academic year begin?

Our academic year typically begins in September. We also offer mid-year admissions in January for certain grade levels, subject to availability.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and need-based financial aid. Contact our admissions office for more information about available opportunities.

What extracurricular activities are available?

We offer a wide range of activities including sports, music, drama, debate club, science club, and various cultural activities to support holistic development.

What is your student-teacher ratio?

We maintain small class sizes with an average student-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention and quality education for every student.

Do you provide transportation?

Yes, we offer safe and reliable school bus services covering various routes in Bayelsa State. Contact us for route information and fees.
